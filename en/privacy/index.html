Privacy policy

Privacy
policy.

Last updated: 23 April 2026.

This document describes what data Boost! Agency (hereafter "the Agency") collects, why, how we store it, and what rights you have. We follow the Law of the Republic of Uzbekistan "On Personal Data" No. 547-II of 02.07.2019 and generally accepted international practices.

1. Who processes your data

The personal data operator is Boost! Agency, website boostagency.uz, contact email: andrevdanil@gmail.com.

2. What data we collect

Through the contact form on the site we collect data you provide voluntarily:

  • first and last name;
  • work email;
  • company name and role;
  • phone number (optional);
  • company website (optional);
  • ad-budget range;
  • the text of your message.

Technical data is also collected automatically: IP address, browser type, pages viewed, referral source (UTM tags). This happens through web-analytics systems.

3. Processing purposes

We use your data to:

  • reply to your request and prepare a media plan and proposal;
  • contact you by email, phone, or Telegram to clarify details;
  • maintain business correspondence and a history of our interaction;
  • improve the site and analyze visitor behavior in anonymized form.

We do not share data with third parties for marketing and do not sell it.

4. Legal basis

Processing is carried out on the basis of your consent, which you give by checking the box on the contact form. You may withdraw consent at any time — see "Your rights."

5. Retention period

Data is stored until the project ends or for 3 years from the last interaction — whichever comes first. After that, data is deleted or anonymized.

6. Data transfer and vendors

Data is processed on our platform platform.boostagency.uz and in third-party services we use: email services, CRM, web analytics. Confidentiality agreements are in place with all vendors. Data transfer between systems uses a secure channel (HTTPS / TLS).

7. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • request access to your personal data;
  • require corrections to inaccurate data;
  • request data deletion;
  • withdraw processing consent;
  • receive a machine-readable copy of your data.

To exercise any of these rights, email andrevdanil@gmail.com with the email address you used in the form. We respond within 10 business days.

8. Cookies and web analytics

The site uses cookies and web analytics to operate the interface and analyze traffic. You can disable cookies in your browser — some features may not work correctly as a result.

9. Policy changes

We may update this document. The current date is shown at the top of the page. We additionally notify by email anyone who has submitted a request about material changes.

10. Contact

For personal-data processing questions: andrevdanil@gmail.com, Telegram @boost_agency.
