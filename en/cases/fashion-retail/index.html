What we did

Four steps — six weeks.

01

Audit

We reviewed current buying. Found overspend zones and channels with potential. Agreed on KPIs: ROAS and CPA.

02

Setup

Connected the product feed, built first-party segments, launched display, video, native, and retargeting from a single algorithm.

03

Training

The first 2 weeks were for gathering conversion data. The algorithm rescored bids in real time. CPA dropped step by step.

04

Scale

By week 6 we doubled the budget without raising CPA. ROAS hit ×3.1 and has held for more than 4 months.
